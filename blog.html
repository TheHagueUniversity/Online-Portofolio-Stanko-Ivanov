Project Blog

Here I post my experiences and things I bump into in The Netherlands. The first 3 photos are pictures of the Dutch New year, this is my first new year in the Netherlands and from what I saw it could get quite hectic. People here take New Years very seriously, since there are many different nations in The Hague people also celebrate their countrie's new years according to their local time. This leads to many fireworks all sort of burnt items (bikes, washing machines, fridges, couches). The other pictures are from the Dutch Design week 2019. It was hosted in Eindhoven for a week, sadly I was only able to attend for one day but I had lots of fun. I do consider this blog page as one of my projects too!.
